Projects

Oscanner

Level: Capstone

Oscanner is a QR Code Attendance System with Data Visualization. It provides a comprehensive solution for managing attendance using QR codes and offers intuitive data visualization features.

Implemented Google Auth & Recaptcha V3 for security, Mailtrap & Google SMTP for email testing, and designed UI with TailwindCSS. Utilized Endroid QR Code for QR generation and D3.js for data visualization. Developed backend functionalities with Laravel & Laravel Breeze, incorporated Flatpickr for date & time selection, and extended QR capabilities with Html5 QR Code. Enhanced data management with PHPSpreadSheet & DomPdf, and improved user experience with integrated TUI Calendar & Nominatim for scheduling and address autocomplete.

Tech Stack: PHP, Laravel, D3.js, Laravel Breeze, HTML, CSS, JS, TailwindCSS, Google Auth, Recaptcha V3, Endroid QR Code, Flatpickr, Html5 QR Code, PHPSpreadSheet, DomPdf, TUI Calendar, Nominatim
